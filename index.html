How to Find the Perfect Cereal (As a Journalism Major)

Assembly time: 5-10 hours
Awesome rating: 99.9% awesome

Do you ever just hate procrastinating what cereal to pick from the grocery store? Well I got you covered. Through my painstaking methods of trial and error I have created the perfect list of cereal curated for everyone!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Money
  • Transportation
  • A bowl
  • A spoon
  • Milk
  • Cereal

  1. Write down three interesting things about yourself.

    Your personality type is imperative to find the perfect cereal for you!

  2. Understanding what cereal correlates most with your personality.

    Ex. If you like that outdoors you probably like bland off-brand cornflake cereal. If your personality is bright, you might like trix. If you are negative person you might like lucky charms.

    A bowl of cornflakes with some fruit toppings for added flavor.

  3. If you are feeling your personality is not enough, select three random cereals to try.

    Of the three you like most, that cereal or the ones similiar in texture are your favorite.

  4. That was general, but if you want to get even more specific. Cereal isolationism is the route for you.

    Once you found a cereal you like through vigorous methods of choosing, never try another brand of cereal again.

  5. Now that you have found a cereal you will stick with for life, enjoy!

    Pull up a chair, pour HALF A CUP of milk, as the cereal sets most accurately with that much milk, and enjoy :)
